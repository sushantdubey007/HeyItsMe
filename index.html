Development of Set-Top-Box Technology

We all are familiar with Set Top Box (STB), driving our cable and dish connections to TV. STB receives digital signals as input and then decode and display contents on a TV. The signal to the STB can either be a television signal or even Internet data via a cable connection. User can also send requests for video on demand, record shows and play it back on TV through STB.
